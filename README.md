## 📦 E-Commerce Sales & Logistics Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

Analyzed large-scale e-commerce data to uncover sales and logistics insights

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard for business monitoring and decision-making

Focused on revenue trends, order patterns, and delivery performance

## 🎯 Objectives

Identify top-performing product categories and revenue drivers

Analyze order trends and detect delays in delivery

Understand customer payment behavior

Provide insights to improve logistics and operational efficiency

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

SQL – Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis

Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization

Excel / CSV – Data storage and preprocessing

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

Sales analysis across product categories and time periods

Identification of delayed orders and trend patterns

Payment method analysis to understand customer preferences

Order volume and revenue tracking across different time frames

## 🔍 Key Insights

Health & Beauty category generated the highest revenue (~1.43M)

Delayed orders showed a significant spike, peaking at 5,676

Majority of transactions (~74%) were made using credit cards

Seasonal and quarterly trends impacted both sales and delivery performance
